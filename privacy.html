SMART UNIVERSE (SUC)

Privacy Policy

How Smart Universe protects your data in the decentralized world.

Last Updated: March 2026

Smart Universe (SUC) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you interact with our decentralized ecosystem.

1. Data We Collect

We collect and process data necessary to provide a secure Web3 experience:

  • Identity Data: Full name and your unique SUC-ID.
  • Contact Data: Email address and mobile number for account security and OTP verification.
  • Blockchain Data: Public Web3 Wallet addresses (MetaMask/Trust Wallet). Important: We never ask for or store your private keys or seed phrases.
  • Transaction Data: Details of SUC Token purchases, staking history, and matrix earnings.

2. Use of Data

Your data is used strictly to maintain the integrity of the Smart Universe platform:

  • To manage your registration and authenticate your SUC-ID.
  • To calculate and distribute Matrix spillover and Stake ROI earnings.
  • To provide 24/7 Customer Support and resolve ticket issues.
  • To prevent fraudulent multiple accounts and bot activities.

3. Data Security & Encryption

We use high-level encryption and decentralized storage methods to ensure your information is safe. Access to your personal data is strictly limited to necessary administrative personnel. However, please remember that transactions on the blockchain are public and irreversible by nature.

4. Third-Party Services

Our platform interacts with external Web3 providers (like Binance Smart Chain, MetaMask, and TradingView). We do not control how these third-party platforms handle data, and we encourage you to read their specific privacy policies.

5. Policy Updates

Smart Universe reserves the right to update this policy as we transition into the Metaverse and expand our SUC Token utilities. Continued use of the platform constitutes your agreement to the updated terms.
